How agency configuration works: MedicLog uses .mlconfig files — encrypted JSON bundles that carry your agency name, unit number, formulary, incident settings, and more. Generate one with the Settings Creator, then distribute it as a QR code, email attachment, or AirDrop. Medics scan once and their app is configured instantly. Settings can be pushed again at any time to update the fleet.
