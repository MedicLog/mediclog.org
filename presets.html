EMR

Emergency Medical Responder

Entry-level certification focused on vital signs assessment and basic life support. No medication administration.

Vitals
  • All vital signs monitoring
Airways
  • OPA / NPA
  • No advanced airways
Cardiac
  • CPR
  • AED
Medications
  • No medications
IV/IO Access
  • None
Medication Timers
  • OFF

EMT-Basic

Emergency Medical Technician

Standard EMT certification with fixed-dose medications and BLS interventions. Foundation of prehospital care.

Vitals
  • All vital signs monitoring
Airways
  • OPA / NPA
  • BVM
  • No supraglottic / ETT
Cardiac
  • CPR
  • AED
Medications
  • Aspirin 324mg
  • Epinephrine auto-injector
  • Oxygen
  • Oral glucose
  • Naloxone (Narcan)
IV/IO Access
  • None
Medication Timers
  • OFF
mediclog://config?preset=emt-basic

AEMT

Advanced Emergency Medical Technician

NREMT Advanced EMT with IV access and limited ALS medications. Bridge between EMT and Paramedic scope.

Vitals
  • All vital signs monitoring
Airways
  • OPA / NPA
  • BVM
  • Supraglottic (iGel, King)
  • No ETT
Cardiac
  • CPR
  • AED
Medications
  • All EMT-Basic meds
  • Limited ALS meds (state-dependent)
  • Some calculated doses may apply
IV/IO Access
  • IV access
Medication Timers
  • ON
mediclog://config?preset=aemt

EMT-Varianced

EMT-IV / Expanded Scope

State variance or expanded scope EMT with IV access and fixed-dose medications only. No weight-based dosing calculations.

Vitals
  • All vital signs monitoring
Airways
  • OPA / NPA
  • BVM
  • Supraglottic (iGel, King)
  • No ETT / surgical airways
Cardiac
  • CPR
  • AED
  • No manual defib / pacing
Medications (Fixed-Dose Only)
  • Naloxone 4mg IN
  • Epinephrine 0.3mg IM
  • Ondansetron 4mg ODT
  • Aspirin
  • Oral glucose
  • No mg/kg or titrated meds
IV/IO Access
  • IV access
  • IO access
Medication Timers
  • ON
mediclog://config?preset=emt-varianced

Paramedic

NRP / Licensed Paramedic

Full ALS scope with complete formulary, advanced airways, and comprehensive cardiac interventions including 12-lead interpretation.

Vitals
  • All vital signs monitoring
Airways
  • OPA / NPA
  • BVM
  • Supraglottic
  • ETT / intubation
  • Surgical cricothyrotomy
Cardiac
  • CPR
  • AED
  • Manual defibrillation
  • Synchronized cardioversion
  • Transcutaneous pacing
  • 12-lead ECG
Medications
  • Full formulary
  • Weight-based dosing
  • Titrated infusions
IV/IO Access
  • IV access
  • IO access
Medication Timers
  • ON

Critical Care / Flight

CCP / FP-C / CFRN

Extended scope for critical care transport and flight medicine. Includes pressors, sedation, paralytics, blood products, and ventilator management.

Vitals
  • All vital signs monitoring
  • Invasive monitoring (art lines)
Airways
  • All paramedic airways
  • Ventilator management
  • RSI / DSI
Cardiac
  • All paramedic cardiac
  • Advanced hemodynamic monitoring
Medications
  • Full paramedic formulary
  • Vasopressors / inotropes
  • Sedation / analgesia infusions
  • Paralytics (RSI)
  • Blood products
IV/IO Access
  • IV access
  • IO access
  • Central line management
Medication Timers
  • ON
